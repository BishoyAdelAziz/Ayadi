Founder’s speech

Founder’s
speech

With over 30 years of experience in the plastic manufacturing sector, my journey began in Saudi Arabia, where I established my first trading company. This venture provided invaluable insights into the industry’s dynamics and underscored the critical importance of quality in water systems.

Recognizing the need for superior water system components, I transitioned from trading to manufacturing, leading to the establishment of our factory in Egypt. Our mission is clear: to innovate and produce top-tier compression fittings that enhance water distribution systems, ensuring efficiency and reliability.

Our commitment extends beyond production. We are deeply invested in the evolution of water systems, striving to develop solutions that not only meet current demands but also anticipate future challenges. This dedication has propelled our growth, allowing us to expand our operations and make a tangible impact in the industry we have integrated sustainable practices into our manufacturing processes, ensuring that our operations contribute positively to the environment. By adopting eco-friendly technologies and minimizing waste, we aim to set a benchmark in the industry for environmental responsibility.

As we look to the future, our vision remains steadfast: to continue driving innovation in water system solutions, to expand our reach, and to uphold our commitment to environmental sustainability. Together with our dedicated team and valued partners, we aspire to make a lasting contribution to the development of human society and the preservation of our planet.
