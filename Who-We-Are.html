About Us

About
Ayadi

Ayadi International for Plastic Industry has a long history and professional manufacturer of pipe fittings. Ayadi was established in 2018 and is located in Alexandria, Egypt, operating under the “EVK” brand. It specializes in the manufacturing of high-quality compression fittings for water systems.

Our products are crafted from premium polypropylene and polyethylene materials, ensuring suitability for applications in drinking water supply, irrigation, and fire-fighting systems.

We take pride in delivering systems that are mechanically robust, capable of withstanding high-water pressure and temperature variations, and designed for ease of installation.

All our products adhere to stringent international standards, positioning us as a reliable choice in the market. Beyond traditional applications, our fittings are also utilized in infrastructure projects and landscaping, demonstrating their versatility and dependability.

In order to make the family use the environmental quality of pipe products, Ayadi adopts imported high-quality raw materials to produce products, to ensure product quality.

Ayadi has strict control & inspection for material incoming inspection, process inspection, and shipment inspection, to ensure product excellence.

Ayadi has more than 100 employees and one outstanding technical and management group. This group has vitality, innovation, and potential, and they can provide customers with quality, efficient service.

Ayadi mainly produces PP compression pipe fittings and other agricultural water-saving irrigation products.

Our products are proudly exported worldwide, including markets such as the Middle East, southern Africa, and Asia etc.

Our Products are widely used in civil water supply buildings, drainage, water supply and heating, home decoration, garden irrigation, agricultural irrigation, and other fields.
