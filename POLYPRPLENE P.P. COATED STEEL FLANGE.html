POLYPRPLENE P.P. COATED STEEL FLANGE

This flange is made of iron and then covered with polypropylene that can withstand a pressure of 16 bars. It is used to connect HDPE pipes and is characterized by being rust-proof, which extends its lifespan on site. It can be used buried or exposed on the ground surface.
