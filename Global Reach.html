Global
Reach

At Ayadi International Plastic Industries, our products are proudly exported to worldwide, including markets such as The Middle East, Southern Africa and, Asia. Our global customer base includes international importers, distributors, and trading companies, who rely on the quality and performance of our fittings for water systems.

Today, Ayadi is recognized as a trusted brand across global markets, and we are actively expanding our international presence through a growing network of exclusive agents and partners. We are committed to delivering exceptional service and long-term value to our clients around the world — and we are always looking for new agents and strategic partners to join our journey.

Ayadi International is here to serve you with dedication, quality, and professionalism.
