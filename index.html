About EVK

Ayadi Group is a Saudi-based company dedicated to manufacturing and trading high-density polyethylene and polypropylene pipe fittings for water systems. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability sets us apart as a trusted name in the industry.

Who we are

Ayadi specializes in the manufacturing of pipe fittings for water systems from polypropylene and polyethylene for drinking water, irrigation, and f irefighting. They pride themselves on providing high-quality systems that are mechanically strong, tolerant to high water pressure and temperature, and easy to install. All products are manufactured according to international standards, making them one of the most reliable options in the market.
