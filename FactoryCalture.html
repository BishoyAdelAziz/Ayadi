Culture

Business
Approach

At AYADI, our business approach is centered around providing high-quality products and services to our customers, while also prioritizing sustainability and innovation. We believe that by focusing on our core values, we can not only meet the needs of our clients but also contribute to the development of the industries we serve. We aim to be a leading player in these industries, while also contributing to the broader societal and environmental goals Of our communities.

Our
Core Values

Quality:

We exceed customer expectations with superior products and services.

Innovation:

Continuous improvement and adaptation to industry advancements.

Sustainability:

Environmentally responsible practices to contribute to a healthier planet.

Our
Vision

To be a globally recognized leader in the plastic industry, specializing in polypropylene and polyethylene fittings. We strive to make safe and sustainable water systems accessible to all by delivering cutting-edge solutions through innovation and excellence.

Mission

At Ayadi, our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and durable plastic fittings and water system solutions that meet international standards. We are committed to supporting infrastructure and development through precision manufacturing, continuous improvement, and exceptional customer service ensuring safe and efficient water connections across all markets we serve.
